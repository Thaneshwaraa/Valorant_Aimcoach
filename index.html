3D Aim Range

A real first-person range rendered in 3D. Your mouse rotates the camera at your actual Valorant sensitivity (0.07°/count), so flicking here feels exactly like the game. Snap to 10 wide targets then 4 tiny ones — we read your overshoot to tell you if your sens is right.

eDPI
DPI is your mouse's hardware speed (set in its software) — it's the same in every game. In-game sens is Valorant's own multiplier on top of it. Multiply them and you get eDPI — your true sensitivity, and the only number that decides how far your view turns per cm of mouse movement. Two players with the same eDPI aim identically even on different DPI.
